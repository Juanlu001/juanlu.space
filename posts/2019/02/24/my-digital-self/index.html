My digital self

Here I am.

Tweets are deleting as we speak. Facebook and Whatsapp are long gone. And, you guessed right, I am back to blogging.

I started a personal blog a long time ago. Learning to program, to write, to love, to learn... It was another life. That blog is no longer online. I don't like leaving traces.

Then I started a programming blog, and then another one. That one was quite famous, a great ride. I learned to teach, to talk to my audience, and many other things along the way.

The present looks kinda gloomy though. Huge corporations owning our data, our digital selves. Yes: our online identity is no longer ours. Are we willing to accept that? This blog starts as my quest to find answers to that question.

See you on the Indie Web.
